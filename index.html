Welcome to Flexo Basketball Camp

Are you an underrated high school player looking for a chance to show off your skills? Join us at the Flexo Basketball Camp, held at the famous Cocoa Beach Facility in Cocoa Beach, Florida!

Dates: July 7th-11th

Location: Cocoa Beach, Florida
